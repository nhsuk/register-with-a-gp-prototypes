Register with {{ practiceName }}

It usually takes about 10 minutes.

You’ll be asked for your:

  • name and address
  • previous name and address
  • contact details
  • date of birth

It helps your GP find any previous medical records if you know the following:

  • your NHS number
  • your previous GPs name and address
  • the date you left or came to the UK (if you’ve lived abroad)
  • your military service personnel number (if you have one) and date you joined the military
How to find your NHS number

An NHS number is a 10 digit number, for example: 943 476 5919

You can find your NHS number:

  • online
  • on a printed prescription
  • on a letter from your GP or a hospital
  • on your NHS medical card if you have one

You can still register without this information.
